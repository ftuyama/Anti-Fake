                                                  Darío Baldi JonD • a year ago

                                                  Hey man, it’s ok to ask questions.

                                                  In some cases I could chose the "image quality" by setting a
                                                  noise threshold (in Luxrender and Vray for example), but in most cases
                                                  it was a subjective choice.

                                                  Comparing samples wasn’t an option (man, I wish I could have done it
                                                  that way!). Cycles, LuxR, Octane and Mitsuba have somewhat similar rendering
                                                  settings, but Maxwell and Vray are just too different in the way you set your rendering settings.

                                                  Indeed I had in mind which integrators worked better, and of course I
                                                  also had the samplers in mind. As I stated, it involved a lot of testing and
                                                  reading each renderer’s documentation. Once you do that it’s fairly simple to
                                                  narrow down your options, find a couple of integrator+sampler combinations
                                                  suited for your scene and just render and compare which combination gives
                                                  better results.

                                                  I thought of putting the settings info I used in each case, but it would
                                                  have been too technical for most readers, and for some cases that wasn’t even feasible:
                                                  In Vray that would mean telling the subdivisions (samples) I set for each
                                                  materials, for the lights, for the sampler, the integrators’ settings (you can
                                                  use 2 at the same time)…

                                                  If you have any other question, please feel free to ask here or to
                                                  contact me.

                                                  Cheers!

                                                                                                                                        Hi Angel, the standard amount of memory on the 970 is 4GB. If you are trying to render a scene that requires more than 4GB, you will run out and receive that message.
